Black Forces Trading Cards

04/28/2024
Watch out Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh and Magic!


Black Forces are hard at work on brand new, hand drawn collectible trading cards. The cards serve as avatars for both members of Black Forces, as well as collaborators. These cards will be printed in limited quantities and issued as pre-order bonuses for purchasing our upcoming EP: Planetary Eclipse.

We look forward to sharing our full vision with all of you when new card types and rare limited prints are ready to be unveiled.
