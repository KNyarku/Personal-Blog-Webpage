Artomatic 2024 Performance

04/06/2024 by OutlandishJones


Thank you DC!

On April 20th, 2024 Black Forces and DJ Woozie performed at Artomatic DC for the very first time. We perfomed some Classics and previewed new tracks currently in progress to a brand new audience. It was an intimate yet energetic performance and we were lucky to be with the presence of new and old fans who took to our music. Check out our gallery of photos from our performance and many of the fine art on display at the event.
